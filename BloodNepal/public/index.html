Process to Donate Blood

To donate blood through Blood Nepal, start by visiting the website and navigating to the "Donate Blood" section. Fill out the registration form with your details, submit it, and await a confirmation email. Review the eligibility criteria provided, then find a nearby donation camp and book an appointment. On the day of donation, prepare by eating well, staying hydrated, and bringing a photo ID. After donating, follow the post-donation care instructions and receive a thank-you email. Optionally, you can get updates on how your blood helps others.

Process to receive blood

To request blood, visit the Blood Nepal website and go to the "Request Blood" section. Fill out the request form with patient details, urgency, and contact information, then submit it. You will receive a confirmation email and possibly a verification call. The system matches your request with potential donors, and you will receive their contact details if a match is found. Coordinate with the donor for the blood donation at a hospital or blood bank. A follow-up email ensures your request is fulfilled, with an option to provide feedback.
